# Changelog
---
**v0.1.0 (2026-07-04) — First Public Release**
Introduces Decision Gate, a runtime decision framework for AI agents.
Highlights: Three-layer architecture (Decision Policy, Health
Assessment, Trigger Engine). Decision Policy governing layer
overriding all routing. Three Health Dimensions: Execution,
Requirement, Decision. Nine routing outputs in three categories.
TRIGGER_DECISION = f(Threshold, Confidence, Policy). Codex
reference implementation. Full documentation in docs/. Example
scenarios: scraping, debugging, coding, research.
---
